GoT Books to Series

While proposing projects, this idea was rejected by editors for low audience to effort ratio. Thus Alyssa and I decided to create it on our own.

We watched every episode and took screenshots and duration timings for the barcharts, and referred to a fanpage as well as read the first book for the differences between the books and the series.

With google analytics tracking and promotion on various sites like Reddit and Flowingdata, it has garnered more than 15,000 page views to date.

Created with VueJS, D3

First built in March 2019 (For the season 8 premiere)
