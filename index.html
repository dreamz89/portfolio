Creativity + Technology

Hi I’m Jocelyn! I’m a front end web developer who graduated from General Assembly's intensive 3 month web development course. The course started us off on the fundamentals of web development as well as touched on various technologies like Javascript, NodeJS, Ruby and ReactJS.

Thereafter, I did an internship where I helped create a data visualization dashboard for Kantar market research agency based on their purchase funnel data.

Currently, I am working in the Straits Times' Interactive Graphics team, where I am surrounded by a team of nice developers and designers, and am as close to creativity as a developer can be.

Visuals make me excited and thus I am happy creating images on Photoshop / Illustrator and animating websites with Javascript. I often browse award winning websites for inspiration for my next project proposal.

In my free time I learn foreign languages, try out new things like painting and ukelele and fly off to various beaches and exotic places.
